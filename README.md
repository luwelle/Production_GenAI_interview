## 📝 About Production_GenAI_interview

This application provides a collection of carefully crafted AI interview questions. These questions focus on real production scenarios, rather than just theory. They cover challenges faced when deploying AI systems in business environments.

Whether you are preparing for an AI job interview or want to understand production AI issues, this tool offers practical content. The questions span multiple difficulty levels, starting from entry-level concepts.

You do not need any programming skill. The app presents the questions in a simple and accessible way. It works well on Windows machines.

The app shows AI interview questions sorted by difficulty level. You can choose from the following:
